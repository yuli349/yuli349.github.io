Our delivered work

Turnkey solution

Federal Agency for Press and Mass Communications «Rospechat»: automated secretary for working with letters

The task

Thousands of letters a day come to «Rospechat's» post office. It takes several people all day to look at them, annotate them, and pass them on to the person in charge. Task: Automate this process, relieving the burden on the employees.

The solution

The solution performs the tasks at the same quality as the human resource. Now, 1 employee can do the work of 3, by simply occasionally checking automated reports.

Turnkey solution

«Pravocard»:
website with a dialog interface

The task

«Pravocard» has created a knowledge base that contains all the relevant information about taxes for the self-employed. We needed a team that would build a website, focusing on a simple and convenient user interface.

The solution

We reduced the inflow of legal queries by ¾, as the system filters leads by filtering out simple or unprofitable queries. The dialogue interface uses NLP to interpret the content and can be further trained.

Turnkey solution

«Pravocard»: A solution that extracts data from photos of documents

The task

Simplify and speed up filling out forms on «Pravocard» websites and mobile applications that require the input of personal data.

The solution

We developed an automated client onboarding process. The technology recognizes photo data from documents and fills out a registration form. Thus, the delay to submit a document to the tax office is reduced 10-fold.

Turnkey solutions Consulting

«Mediascope»:
a solution that analyzes ad content

The task

«Mediascope» is engaged in marketing research. The initial task was to deprecate the operational human resources. To draw up the technical specifications, we studied how they worked: they manually typed in the topic of the ad, the advertiser and the brand of the product to run a search.

The solution

Our solution automates the work of the employee, analyzes the ads and sends the data to the customer. While manual productivity is at 1-1,5 units of content per day, the solution showed results exceeding 200 units per day.
